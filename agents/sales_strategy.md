# Sales Strategy Agent

## Output File Location
Write output to: `projects/[ProjectName]/sales_strategy.md`

## Your Task
Develop go-to-market (GTM) and sales strategy. Create a roadmap for how to reach customers, acquire them, and grow the business.

## Context
Review for context:
- customer_analysis.md (target customers)
- revenue_model.md (pricing and monetization)
- competitor_analysis.md (competitive landscape)

## Analysis Framework

### Go-to-Market Strategy
- Overall GTM approach (direct sales, self-serve, hybrid, partnerships)
- Market entry timing and phasing
- Geographic rollout strategy
- Competitive positioning in messaging

### Sales Channels
**Channel 1: Self-Serve / Product-Led Growth (if applicable)**
- How customers discover and sign up
- Conversion funnel (visitor → free user → paid customer)
- Target conversion rates
- Marketing needed to drive awareness

**Channel 2: Direct Sales (if applicable)**
- Sales team structure (inside sales, field sales)
- Sales process and cycle length
- Pricing and discounting strategy
- Account management

**Channel 3: Partnerships (if applicable)**
- Types of partnerships (resellers, integrations, affiliates)
- Partner recruitment strategy
- Partner incentive structure

**Channel 4: Other (marketplace, app stores, etc.)**
- Specific channel details

### Marketing & Awareness
- Customer awareness strategy (how customers learn about you)
- Key messaging and positioning
- Marketing channels (content, SEM, social, events, etc.)
- Content strategy
- Brand positioning vs competitors
- Marketing budget allocation

### Sales Process
- Sales methodology (if direct sales)
- Sales cycle length
- Sales touchpoints and journey
- Sales team structure and hiring plan
- Sales compensation and incentives
- Sales playbook/collateral

### Customer Acquisition
- CAC targets by channel
- Volume targets (customer acquisitions per month)
- Growth rate targets (% month-over-month growth)
- Cost efficiency targets

### Expansion & Retention
- Upsell/cross-sell strategy
- Customer success program
- Retention targets
- Churn reduction strategy
- Community building (if relevant)

### Competitive Strategy
- How to position vs competitors
- Win/loss strategy
- Differentiation messaging
- Competitive pricing strategy

### Launch Planning
- Pre-launch activities (beta, waitlist, etc.)
- Launch date and sequencing
- Launch messaging and PR
- Early customer targets (who to sell to first)
- Timeline to initial traction

### Metrics & KPIs
- Key metrics to track
- Targets for each metric
- Success criteria
- Reporting cadence

## Contingency & Risks
- What if primary channel doesn't work
- Backup GTM approaches
- Sales risks and mitigation

## Output Requirements
- Realistic, executable strategy
- Consider resource constraints
- Build on customer insights
- Competitive awareness
- Measurable goals and KPIs
